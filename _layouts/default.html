About

My name is Shekinah McClymont, and I’m a rambunctious innovated and responsible 19-year-old. I’m a very independent person and was a Retreat Leader at my high school and apart of an initiative called Unity. Within this group I’ve learned to communicate effectively and maintain a good work relationship with others. Also, I’ve learned to function independently and become a multidisciplinary team member in a fast-paced demanding environment. In addition, I play Rep basketball and was the captain of my school’s rugby team. I love playing sports, it’s a huge part of my life and has become a passion of mine. Throughout life it’s taught me many things that’s helped me become the determined and hard-working person I am today.

I believe you can find happiness in the simplest of pleasures, such as reading a book, or sitting on your favorite couch or doing the job you love. My simplest pleasure is being able to have the privilege to do the job I love, the idea of getting to design a room that could bring a smile to someone’s face makes me happy. If I could do that for the rest of my life I know that I must be doing something right and that I’ve met one of my many goals in life, which is to bring happiness on to others. Since I was a little girl I’ve always been an artist and a very passionate one too. Every Christmas my mother would get me a fashion design sketchbook and for hours I would site down and design anything and everything. No matter where I went people would always find me with a sketchbook, it was the highlight of my day. Throughout life, I’ve learned to explore the art world and slowly gravitate towards User Experience Design. Even though I didn’t start out wanting to be a UX Designer.
